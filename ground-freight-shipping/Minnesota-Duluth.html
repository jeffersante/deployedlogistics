Freight Shipping Duluth, Minnesota

Whether using Minnesota Highway 61, 194 or 23, or I-35 to move cargo through Duluth, our trusted carrier network will work closely with you to develop a complete freight forwarding solution that meets the needs of your business and your cargo. It is from this seaport city on Lake Superior that we can provide preferred carrier partners to get your cargo into transport with ground freight shipping, both less than truckload or full truckload shipping. We also offer secure add-on services you may require such as freight special handling, late night or after hours deliveries, palletizing, and work with custom brokers. Whether using arterial surface roads that emanate from the city such as County Road 3, 4, 6, 9, 17 or 32 or Boundary Avenue or Midway Road, or one of the many others, we can manage, move and stay vigilant for all your logistics needs and give you the peace of mind we are the choice for the most demanding shippers.

Deployed Logistics Inc
Deployed Logistics has three decades of experience with freight forwarding managing less-than-truckload, full truckload and intermodal shipping for thousands of commercial customers. Real-time tracking, customer service staff always available by phone or email, expedited shipping, palletizing, warehousing and special handling available. Licensed as a NVOCC, Indirect Air Carrier, Freight Forwarder and Motor Carrier with one of the largest transport networks for ground, ocean or air freight.
