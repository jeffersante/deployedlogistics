Freight Shipping Pocatello, Idaho

Shipping to and from this fifth largest city and the county seat in Idaho, is made easier with major Interstate Highways 15, 84, 184, US Route 91 and 30. We know the many ancillary roadways that provide infrastructure support which enable the Deployed Logistics team to manage, move and distribute freight for the most demanding companies in Pocatello and throughout the country. Our carrier partners have knowledge of the rail network, aviation support and freight transport corridors that give you the peace of mind your cargo is safe. Using advanced tools such as real-time tracking and a highly available customer care team, you have the assurance and knowledge you need so you know where your cargo shipment is every step of the way. We are also able to readily manage any of the additional add-on services you may require such as freight special handling, late night or after hours deliveries, palletizing, and work with custom brokers.

Deployed Logistics has three decades of experience with freight forwarding managing less-than-truckload, full truckload and intermodal shipping for thousands of commercial customers. Real-time tracking, customer service staff always available by phone or email, expedited shipping, palletizing, warehousing and special handling available.
