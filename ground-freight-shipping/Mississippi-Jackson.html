Freight Shipping Jackson, Mississippi

Known to be the capitol city on the Pearl River in Mississippi, and home to an extensive roadway grid, including I-20, I-55, I-220 along with US Highways 49, 51, 80 and State Highways 18, 25 and the Natchez Trace Parkway, enables those in Jackson access to the Eastern Seaboard and gives the Deployed Logistics transport network the ability to take advantage of competitive shipping freight rates with our preferred carrier’s status in this southern US metropolis. Whether using one of the many advanced systems we use for real-time tracking or our team available by phone or email, your cargo moving efforts can be a trusted experience when you work with us. With expansive freight resources, we can offer peace of mind to move freight and handle any last-minute or unexpected need, including warehousing, palletizing and after-hours delivery if needed. If you need to move or manage freight, Deployed Logistics gives you the peace of mind as the choice of the most demanding shippers.

Deployed Logistics Inc
Deployed Logistics has three decades of experience with freight forwarding managing less-than-truckload, full truckload and intermodal shipping for thousands of commercial customers. Real-time tracking, customer service staff always available by phone or email, expedited shipping, palletizing, warehousing and special handling available. Licensed as a NVOCC, Indirect Air Carrier, Freight Forwarder and Motor Carrier with one of the largest transport networks for ground, ocean or air freight.
