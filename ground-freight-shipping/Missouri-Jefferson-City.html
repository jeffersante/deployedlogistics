Freight Shipping Jefferson City, Missouri

Shipping to and from Jefferson City, this capital city in Missouri, is made easier with major Interstate Highway I-70 passing nearby, and U.S. Routes 50, 54, 63 and State Routes 179 and 94. We know the many ancillary roadways that provide infrastructure support which enable the Deployed Logistics team to manage, move and distribute freight for the most demanding companies. Our carrier partners have knowledge of the rail network, aviation support and freight transport corridors that give you the peace of mind your cargo is safe. Using advanced tools such as real-time tracking and a highly available customer care team whenever you have questions, you have the assurance and knowledge you need so you know where your cargo shipment is every step of the way. We are also able to readily manage any of the additional add-on services you may require such as freight special handling, late night or after hours deliveries, palletizing, and work with custom brokers.
