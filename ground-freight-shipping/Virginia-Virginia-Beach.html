Freight Shipping Virginia Beach, Virginia

Shipping to and from Virginia Beach, Virginia, this resort city with miles of beaches, is made easier with major Interstate Highways I-64 via I-264, I-664, US Route 58, US Route 60, US Route 13 and State Route 165. Freight forwarding is also made easier with the Chesapeake Bay Bridge-Tunnel, the longest bridge-tunnel in the world, all of which enable the Deployed Logistics team to manage, move and distribute freight for the most demanding companies. Our carrier partners have knowledge of the roadways, rail network, aviation support and freight transport corridors that give you the peace of mind your cargo is safe. Using advanced tools such as real-time tracking and a highly available customer care team whenever you have questions, you have the assurance and knowledge you need so you know where your cargo shipment is every step of the way. Let us start the process with a competitive quote and the help of our customer care team.
