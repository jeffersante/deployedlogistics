Freight Shipping Gaithersburg, Maryland

Freight shipping in Gaithersburg, one of the largest Maryland cities by population divided into east and west sections by I-270, can be an experience with peace of mind using the Deployed Logistics team. With a primary arterial road network led by Frederick Avenue, Diamond Avenue, and Quince Orchard Road, and the ability to connect to the Capital Beltway, I-370 and Intercounty Connector linked to I-95, our carrier network has access to the expansive United States and global destinations. The Deployed Logistics customer care team will use the abundance of resources and be able to provide special services such as warehousing. If your shipment requires time sensitive delivery, we can arrange for it. And, if palletizing will make a difference to you and reduce your freight shipping costs, we can manage that. No matter which industry in Gaithersburg you are in or how extensive your logistics needs are, the Deployed Logistics team is dedicated to serving you and being available for all your cargo moving needs.

Deployed Logistics Inc
Deployed Logistics has three decades of experience with freight forwarding managing less-than-truckload, full truckload and intermodal shipping for thousands of commercial customers. Real-time tracking, customer service staff always available by phone or email, expedited shipping, palletizing, warehousing and special handling available. Licensed as a NVOCC, Indirect Air Carrier, Freight Forwarder and Motor Carrier with one of the largest transport networks for ground, ocean or air freight.
