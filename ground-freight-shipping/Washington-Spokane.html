Freight Shipping Spokane, Washington

On the Spokane River, west of the Rocky Mountain foothills, shipping to and from Spokane is made easier with a dedicated team with three decades’ experience creating complete logistics solutions. Once an important rail and shipping center because of its setting between historical mining and farming areas, freight shipping can be made easier with Interstate Highway I-90 and highways US 195, 395 and 2. The North Spokane Corridor construction is still underway as a $2.2B limited access highway project. The Deployed Logistics team brings online technology tools that allow for real-time tracking of freight shipments of every type. Whether shipping to Spokane or from it, the Deployed Logistics partner network of the most trusted transporters means your logistics needs will be managed, even if they include additional services such as palletizing, warehousing, late-night or after-hours deliveries or special handling. Start the process now with a competitive bid for economical freight forwarding.
